About EAM Consulting

EAM Consulting is a Management Consulting Firm, specializing in the internationalization of small and medium-sized Enterprises. She has more than 17 years of experience in Assisting and Advising international companies and public institutions.

Our History

Eddouks Automotive managment Consulting was founded at two locations:
01.07.2010 based in Kenitra, Morocco
01.07.2018 with a 2nd. base in Dusseldorf, Germany

The first location is Kenitra. A geographically well-situated city with easy access to Casablanca, Rabat and Tangier. Kenitra has an industrial platform as a free trade zone ''AFZ: Atlantic Free Zone'' and becomes the new Mecca of the moroccan automotive industry. PSA is building a factory there, that will deliver the first cars from 2021 onwards.

The second location is Dusseldorf. A vibrant metropolis with remarkable economic growth and very good quality of life. Here, the consultant industry has etablished very well. Geographically, Düsseldorf is in the middle of Germany, from there, the connection to D/A/CH and Morocco is very favorable.

Our Focus

Our focus is on Business Development & Sale, Marketing Consulting, Interim- and Project Management.
